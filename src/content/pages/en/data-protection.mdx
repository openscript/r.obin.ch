## How do we collect your data?

This website does not use cookies or other markers to track visitors. For visitor analytics, a hash value is generated from the visitor's IP address and User-Agent. This hash value is recalculated daily and serves as a unique visitor ID for the day. The IP address is anonymized in the process and can no longer be traced back by the website operator.

## How will we use your data?

This website collects your data so it's possible to analyze the usage of the website.

## How do we store your data?

This website securely stores your data at an Oracle server farm in Switzerland.

## What are your data protection rights?

As the data is collected completely anonymously, you have no data protection rights in this context as we cannot identify you within the stored data.
